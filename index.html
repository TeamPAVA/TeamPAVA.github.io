Praise from Critically Acclaimed Actor, Patrick Stewart

PAVA is an absolute dream team. I first met PAVA, or "The PAVA" as I call them, at an audition for 2001: A Space Odyssey. Yes, you read that correctly, their brief stint in cinema brought you the cinematic masterpiece of the ages. (I unfortunately didn’t get the part, but I digress.) PAVA moved onto to develop the first iPhone prototype before Steve Jobs “acquired” it (His words, not mine.) PAVA believes in clean energy and has contributed miles of code to Tesla’s autopilot software. Fun Fact: At least one of the PAVA team is from the same planet as Elon Musk*, but they don’t like talking about that much... Long story short Team PAVA is no fly-by-night operation. If you are looking for the tried and true, then you, kind person, are without a doubt, looking for PAVA.
