Praise from Critically Acclaimed Actor, Patrick Stewart

PAVAP is an absolute dream team. I first met PAVAP, or "The PAVAP" as I call them, at an audition for 2001: A Space Odyssey. Yes, you read that correctly, their brief stint in cinema brought you the cinematic masterpiece of the ages. (I unfortunately didn’t get the part, but I digress.) PAVAP moved onto to develop the first iPhone prototype before Steve Jobs “acquired” it (His words, not mine.) PAVAP believes in clean energy and has contributed miles of code to Tesla’s autopilot software. Fun Fact: At least one of the PAVAP team is from the same planet as Elon Musk*, but they don’t like talking about that much... Long story short Team PAVAP is no fly-by-night operation. If you are looking for the tried and true, then you, kind person, are without a doubt, looking for PAVAP.
